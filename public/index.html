ABOUT US

Built by job seekers,
for job seekers.

The HR Files exists for one reason: to give candidates the honest, unfiltered information they deserve before walking into an interview room.

Every day, thousands of people spend weeks, sometimes months, going through interview rounds, completing take-home assignments, and showing up fully prepared. Then nothing. No email. No call. No rejection. Just silence.

Some HR teams never send a rejection. Some don't respond after a single-round phone screen. Some go quiet after five rounds and a final panel, leaving candidates in the dark, second-guessing themselves, unable to move on.

We built this platform because that experience is not an accident. It's a pattern. And patterns can be documented, tracked, and exposed.

"Real reviews from real job seekers. No corporate spin. No employer responses. Just what actually happened, so the next person knows what they're walking into."

Every review filed here costs someone six minutes. What it gives the next candidate is something far more valuable: the truth about who they're about to deal with.
